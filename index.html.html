DirectDebit.cc  ·  Phase 8  ·  Updated June 2026  ·  3 Workflows

1Cancellation Initiation & Immediate Collection Impact
Trigger: Ops or merchant clicks Cancel in the mandate actions dropdown on SCR-MND-001  ·  Actors: Ops Team / Merchant · System · RPA
ℹ️ Cancellation can only be initiated from SCR-MND-001 (Mandate Management screen). Only the ops team and merchant can trigger it. Once confirmed, cancellation is irreversible — there is no undo.
ℹ️ The system takes immediate action on all active collections the moment cancellation is confirmed. Different collection statuses are handled differently — see the collection impact section below.
⚠️ Cancellation is permanent and irreversible. Once the ops team or merchant confirms the cancellation dialog, the process cannot be stopped or reversed from any screen.

WF 1b — Pending Bank Response Collection: What Happens After Bank Responds
ℹ️ When cancellation is initiated and a collection is in Pending Bank Response status, the system cannot cancel the collection immediately — the bank is processing it. Phase 6 processes the settlement file as normal, and once the bank responds the system applies cancellation logic.

2RPA Cancellation File Submission & Bank Response
Actors: RPA · Bank · System  ·  Fully automated · No human action required after initiation · Continues from WF 1
ℹ️ Once all active collections have been resolved (WF 1), the RPA submits the cancellation file to the bank. The bank either confirms the cancellation or rejects it due to wrong timing. If rejected, the system retries automatically — no manual intervention is needed at any point.
ℹ️ Wrong timing is the only reason the bank rejects a cancellation. The system waits and resubmits automatically until the bank accepts.
